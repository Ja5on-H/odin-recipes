Peanut Butter Fingers

A quick and convenient snack for those in a rush

Peanut butter fingers are the brain child of Joey Tribbiani and are a perfect snack if you're short on time

Ingredients

  • Jar of peanut butter. Smooth or crunchy

Steps

  1. Open jar of peanut butter
  2. Insert 1 or 2 fingers (depending on appetite) into jar and scrape up peanut butter
  3. Place fingers in mouth and enjoy
