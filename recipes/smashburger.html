Smash burger

A quick, easy and delicious smash burger, homemade in minutes.

Smash burgers are formed into balls of ground beef then pressed or smashed with a burger press into a thin patty as soon as they hit the grill. This creates extra browning and flavor on the outside while maintaining the patty's juiciness.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (the fattier the meat, the juicier the burger)
  • Sauce. A simple sauce made with mayo, mustard, onions and pickles
  • Brioche buns
  • Sliced cheddar cheese
  • Toppings. Pickles, onion, lettuce and tomato

Steps

  1. Prepare patties. Form 2 quarter pounder balls
  2. Combine mayo, mustard, onions and pickles to make your sauce
  3. Slice lettuce, onions, pickles and tomatoes ready to assemble just before your patties are done
  4. Toast and butter buns
  5. Heat a little oil over medium and once hot place your beef balls into the pan and smash each ball into a thin patty using a spatula. Sear for 2 minutes then flip and place cheese slice onto cooked patty cooking for a further minute
  6. Stack both patties on top of each other and add to toasted bun. Finally add lettuce, tomato, onion and pickles
  7. Wolf down
